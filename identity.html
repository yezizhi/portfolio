identityABC

American-born Chinese children may have difficulty learning Chinese due to the lack of an environment.

identityABC is a Chinese learning app that helps them learn Chinese more easily by learning through games and provide a supportive community that brings family and friends together.


My Role

Research, UX/UI design, Branding

Problem

Language learning is a very important part of a child's identity development. However, American-born Chinese children may have difficulty learning Chinese due to the lack of an environment.

Solution

Our Chinese learning app will make learning Chinese easier by learning through games, learning with friends and getting parental support. It provides:

A tool to help them learn chinese with fun;

A community gathering friends who learn chinese;

A method that let children get rewards for their achievement from their parents.

Target Market

Name: John

Age: 9

He was born in the U.S. and his parents are chinese working in the U.S.

He speaks English at school and Mandarin at home. His classmates have a variety of ethnic, the majority are white americans.

He takes Mandarin after school classes, but become a little bored of learning chinese characters.

Name: Susan

Age: 35

She was born in China, grew up in China, but is now working in the U.S. She has a son of 9 years old, who was born in the U.S.

She speaks English at workl but Mandarin at home. She values Chinese traditions, and concerns about the child's education.

Name: Zhenghe

Age: 70

He retired and lives in China with his wife. He has a grandson of 9 years old who was born and living in the U.S.

He has a hobby of learning English. He usually has video calls with his grandson every week, and is looking forward to see his grandson on the summer vacation.

Feedback

  • The button “Games” should be bigger and easy to find.
  • There need to have a "YOU WIN!" message after users win the game.
  • There need a game list page.
  • Ranking should be related to each game.
  • There need to have a "Add child" function.
  • Children can only be added after approved.
  • Forum need a "comment" function to make it more like a community.
