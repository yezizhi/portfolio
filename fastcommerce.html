FastCommerce

FastCommerce is an e-commerce platform that provides tens of thousands of merchants with an all-in-one commerce platform to start and grow their businesses.


My Role

Research, UX/UI design, Branding

Problem

How might we provide small business owners who want to sell online an all inclusive but easy to use e-commerce platform?

Principles

All inclusive

Everything you need to to sell online, including Inventory control, order processing, payments and shipping.

Easy to use

  • No technical skills needed.
  • Fully customizable.
  • Hold your hands every step of the way.

User Persona

Joana

35 years old | Austin, TX

Joanna owns a local small business selling sandals of her own design. She now wants to move her business online.

She knew how to sell offline, but had no experience selling online. Therefore, she hopes the app will be easy for beginners to use.

Our Solutions

Combine selection and creation functions:

  • Combine select and create into one input box;
  • Save the value entered by the user in the drop-down menu so they can select it next time.

Provides users with 3 ways to create swatches:

  • Use hex color code;
  • Upload an image/Add URL;
  • Select variant image.
