THREE-BODYER

THREE-BODYER is a Science Fiction Fans Community.

The "Three-Body Problem" is a series of science ﬁction novel. It has a lot of fans, but doesn’t have an independent community.

We made an online community for the fans. We designed the logo, made a brand, conducted a campaign, and made a website for them to discuss the novel, create new novels and films, and sell or buy goods they created.


My Role

Research, UX/UI design, Branding
